Seeking Feedback

Seeking Feedback

brief definition

ACTIVITY: Grow and Glow

Let's draw a picture in 15 seconds.

If asked, give feedback using the "Glow & Grow" method:

  • GLOW → Something they did well
  • GROW → One way they could it

LEARNING

Giving and receiving feedback helps us grow by recognising out strengths ("Glows") and areas to improve ("Grows").

Practicing this builds self-awreness, confidence, and a mindset that sees feedback as a tool for learning, not criticism.
