Values, Traits & Strengths

Values = What matters most to you

Character Traits = Who you are

Strengths = What you’re good at

Values, Traits & Strengths

Values

Values are the principles and beliefs that guide your choices and actions . They’re about what’s important to you in life - like kindness, honesty, or freedom.

Character Traits

Character traits describe your personality, the qualities that make you, you. They show how you usually behave and interact with people - like being patient, friendly, or determined.

Strengths

Strengths are your skills and abilities, the things you’re good at. They might be natural talents or things you’ve practiced, like solving problems, listening, or staying calm.
