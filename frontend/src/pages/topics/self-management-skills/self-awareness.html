Self Awareness

Self awareness is understanding your thoughts, feelings, and behaviours

It's like looking in a mirror and seeing youself clearly, knowing what you're good at, what you need to work on, abnd how you affect others

It's a bit like being your own detective, noticing and understanding what makes you, you

Our Favourite 6 Skills

Self-Compassion

Talk to yourself with the same kindness you would a best friend

Emotional Awareness

Pay attention to your thoughts and feelings to understand yourself better.
Name your feelings to tame your feelings

Honesty

Telling the truth to yourself about how you feel and what you do

Seeking Feedback

Ask for and listen to feedback from others to help you grow

Mindfulness

Stay present and aware of your emotions

Self Reflection

Reflect on your experiences and learn from them to grow
