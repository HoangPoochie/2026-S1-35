App Structure
A two-view architecture:
1. Home Dashboard — the landing page. Shows the Introduction module 
prominently at the top, then the 6 modules as cards below, 
accessible in any order. Each card shows the module name, a
short description, and a visual icon consistent with the BVM branding.

2. Module View — when a module is selected, it opens in a clean 
full-screen reader. Content is broken into "screens" (not slides — 
more on this below), navigated with next/back arrows or a progress bar. 
Students on their own devices land on the same module but can tap ahead or 
revisit freely.

Design Language (from the slides)

Deep teal (#1a5276 range) as the primary colour
Slate blue-grey as the secondary/accent panel colour
Italic serif font for headings (e.g. Playfair Display or similar from Google Fonts)
Clean sans-serif for body text
Dot motifs as decorative elements
White backgrounds with generous whitespace
